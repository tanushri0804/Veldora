Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Yes, we offer airport transportation services for our guests. Please contact our concierge at least 48 hours prior to your arrival to arrange this service. Additional charges may apply.

We offer valet parking services for $50 per night. Limited self-parking is also available in our underground garage for $35 per night. Parking spaces cannot be reserved in advance.

Reservations can be cancelled free of charge up to 48 hours before your scheduled arrival date. Cancellations made within 48 hours will incur a charge of one night's room rate plus tax.
